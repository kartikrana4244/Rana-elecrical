Our Story

Rana Electrical is a family-run business built on the foundation of trust, quality, and customer satisfaction. For years, we have been serving our local community with dedication and expertise in AC and electrical services.

What started as a small family venture has grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for our commitment to excellence and honest business practices.

Family-Run Business

As a family-run business, we understand the importance of treating every customer like family. Our values of integrity, respect, and quality service are at the heart of everything we do. We take pride in building long-lasting relationships with our customers based on trust and reliability.

Experienced Technicians

Our team consists of highly skilled and certified technicians with extensive experience in AC repair, installation, maintenance, and electrical services. We stay updated with the latest technologies and techniques to provide you with the best possible service.

Each technician undergoes regular training to ensure they can handle any challenge, from simple repairs to complex installations. You can trust our team to get the job done right the first time.

Honest Pricing & Quick Service

We believe in transparency and fairness. Our pricing is straightforward with no hidden charges or surprise fees. We provide detailed estimates before starting any work, so you know exactly what to expect.

Time is valuable, and we respect yours. Our quick response time and efficient service ensure that your AC or electrical issues are resolved promptly, minimizing disruption to your daily life.
